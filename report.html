GOLDMAN SACHS 2021 PEOPLE STRATEGY REPORT

Delivering Excellence Together

As a leading global institution, we’re only as strong as our people. See how being trusted partners in our people’s professional and personal development can unlock their potential and foster exceptional growth — not just for them, but in turn, for our clients, partners, and communities.

Our Vision

Working with the world’s leading businesses, entrepreneurs, and institutions requires world-class talent. In order to deliver outstanding performance to our clients, diverse backgrounds and perspectives are imperative. By bringing our people together with purpose and investing in them, we can continue to drive excellence for years to come.

Developing Our Talent and Supporting Our People

To unlock the potential of our people and teams, we’re dedicated to providing programs and resources that support growth goals. In order to be the best place for talent to work, grow, and thrive, we support our people and their families with world-class offerings.
